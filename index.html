About Us

Welcome

KoKarya is equipped with spacious open cubicles as well as enclosed cabins (6 seater).

Situated adjacent to Marenahalli. Jayanagar Metro station. Walking distance to JP Nagar, Central with public buses on outer ring road running on either side to Whitefield/Electronic City and Banashankari bus stop.

Thinking to work from home, Think KoKarya in the Jayanagar & JP Nagar neighborhood

KoKarya has enormous space for open cubicles, 4 enclosed cabins (6 seater), one on one cabin for quick discussions or interviews, Main conference room (8-10 seater), pantry area and restrooms.

KoKarya is opened for 24/7 with tight security personnels and CCTV cameras installed within the building.
